# Corporate Structure

UltimateTech Group Ltd operates under a **Parent‑Subsidiary Model**, which provides legal separation, risk isolation, and operational focus.

## Parent Entity
**UltimateTech Group Ltd** (Registered in the United Kingdom)
- **Role:** Strategic Governance, Intellectual Property Holding, and Executive Oversight.

## Subsidiary A: UltimateTech Enterprises Ltd
- **Jurisdiction:** United States (CA) / Global Operations
- **Focus:** Enterprise IT Solutions & Hardware Manufacturing
- **Key Asset:** **XT‑Series** Proprietary Payment Terminal Hardware.
- **Function:** This subsidiary is the engineering arm of the Group. It develops the physical and software infrastructure that powers the Group's security posture.

## Subsidiary B: UltimateTech 4reignXchange Ltd
- **Jurisdiction:** United Kingdom
- **Focus:** Fintech & Specialized Liquidity Provision
- **Key Asset:** Proprietary FX aggregation and settlement engine.
- **Function:** Handles multi‑currency settlements (USD, NGN, USDC) and provides the liquidity rail for cross‑border payments. This entity is the gateway for global commerce flow.

## Subsidiary C: UltimateTech Capital Ltd
- **Jurisdiction:** United Kingdom (FCA Framework Adherence)
- **Focus:** Institutional Brokerage & Capital Markets
- **Key Asset:** MetaTrader 5 White Label Infrastructure & Direct Market Access (DMA) connectivity.
- **Function:** Facilitates trading in Forex, Digital Assets, Commodities, and Global Indices for professional and institutional clients.

## Inter‑Subsidiary Synergy
- **UltimateTech Enterprises** builds the secure terminal.
- **4reignXchange** settles the transaction value.
- **UltimateTech Capital** provides the market exposure for the underlying asset.

This closed‑loop system is the proprietary advantage of the UltimateTech Group.
